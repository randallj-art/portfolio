structure / reflection / pattern

My latest oil paintings all celebrate the beautiful, intricate patterns that I find in the interaction between light and complex structures. Because of my interest in structure and in the reflective properties of glass, the subjects of my paintings are often architectural. On this page, I have gathered the paintings I have made that are most relevant to this theme. Please click on any image below to see more information.
