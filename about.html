about

My name is Randall Johanningsmeier, and I am an artist currently based in Omaha, Nebraska. I graduated from Swarthmore College in 2024, with an undergraduate degree in studio art and mathematics.

I primarily make oil paintings these days, but I have often worked with other media and methods such as drawing, acrylic painting, woodworking, printmaking, and quilting. I don't like to limit myself.

My latest projects all celebrate the beautiful, intricate patterns that I find in the interaction between light and complex structures. Because of my interest in structure and in the reflective properties of glass, the subjects of my paintings are often architectural.

When I am not painting or drawing, I spend much of my time with other forms of art: I read, write, act in plays, think deeply about mathematics, and make music. To me, everything is connected.
